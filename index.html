Understanding BMI and Health

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely used screening tool that helps assess whether you're at a healthy weight for your height. While BMI doesn't directly measure body fat, it provides a useful indicator for most adults.

BMI categories are based on extensive research and are used by healthcare professionals worldwide. However, remember that BMI is just one factor in assessing health, and individual circumstances may vary.

The Science of Sleep Cycles

Sleep occurs in cycles of approximately 90 minutes, consisting of light sleep, deep sleep, and REM sleep phases. Waking up at the end of a complete cycle helps you feel more refreshed and alert.

Our calculator recommends bedtimes that align with natural sleep cycles, accounting for the average 15 minutes it takes to fall asleep. Quality sleep is essential for cognitive function, immune health, and overall well-being.
